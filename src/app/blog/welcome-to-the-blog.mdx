# The Data Stories Nobody Tells in our Ecosystem

*How Data Quietly Powers Real Economies — And Why I’m Finally Talking About It.*

For the past eight years, I’ve had a front-row seat to how data drives some of the most critical systems in Nigeria’s economy.

From fintech infrastructure at **OnePipe**, to large-scale banking analytics at **Ecobank**, to energy and mobility solutions at **Sahara Group** — my work has lived at the intersection of data, business, and real-world impact.

Not theory. Not demo projects. Real systems. Real money. Real consequences.

Those are the stories that matter.
